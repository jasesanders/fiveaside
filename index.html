IWVSC 5-A-Side Legacy Tournament

The continuation of a Ridgecrest soccer community staple! The 5-A-Side Legacy Tournament will be Memorial Day Weekend. We are striving to accomplish the same feel, competition and overall spirit as the Matt Armstrong 5-A-Side Tournament run for 20 years here in town.
